About Us

We design, build, test, and ride bikes!!! Many people think this means we put components on a frame. More than that, we design and fabricate the frame from scratch. We even make some custom components. Our club was founded Fall 2021 by Ziven Posner and Jacob Pashman. It has been a long ride, luckily some of us are randonneurs :)

All members are current UC Berkeley students. The club is open to all majors and we welcome everyone. We look for members to have a strong work ethic, good communication. An interest in cycling is certainly a plus.
