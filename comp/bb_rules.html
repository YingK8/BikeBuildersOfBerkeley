The competition consists of the following components

1 | Public Voting - 100%

While bikes are on display in the tent, Sea Otter attendees will be able to inspect the bikes and vote. Voting will likely be done using paper, but may be done online if we can find a secure way to manage it.

2 | Judging - 00%

In the future, bikes will be judged by 3 people who work in the bike industry.
Contenders will be expected to put together a slide deck no longer than 10 slides TOTAL
Up to 3 people from each team will have 10 minutes to present their slides to the judges
See the JUDGING RUBRIC, and a SAMPLE SLIDE

3 | Race - 00%

There will be no race this year, but in the future we would like to have a race in the future worth between 1% and 15% of the overall score
